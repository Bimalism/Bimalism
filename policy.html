1. Introduction

Welcome to our Student Portal. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

By using our website and services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the data practices described, you should not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

When you register or use our services, we may ask you to provide:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Academic information (grade level, school)

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, etc.)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To personalize your experience
  • To process your requests and transactions
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To improve our website and services
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

Note: We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

These measures include:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls to personal information
  • Secure server infrastructure

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information.

Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. They are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the operation of our service
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect information about how you use our website
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver advertisements relevant to you

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

We may share your information with third parties in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., payment processing, data analysis, email delivery).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information where required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred.

We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal information and to treat it in accordance with the law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your personal information.
  • Rectification: The right to request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Erasure: The right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • Restriction: The right to request limiting the processing of your personal information.
  • Data Portability: The right to request transfer of your data to another organization.
  • Objection: The right to object to processing of your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for students, including those under the age of 18. We take children's privacy seriously and encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's online activities.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

For Parents: You may review, update, or delete your child's personal information by contacting us. You may also refuse to allow further collection or use of your child's information.

9. Policy Changes

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

11. Door Delivery Information

As part of our challenge reward system, we collect specific information required for door delivery of gifts and prizes. This information is collected only when you participate in and qualify for our reward programs.

Information Required for Door Delivery

When you qualify for a door delivery reward, we collect the following mandatory information:

  • Complete Delivery Address: Including house number, street, area, landmark
  • City and State: For geographical location verification
  • PIN Code: For accurate delivery routing
  • Contact Phone Number: For delivery coordination and confirmation
  • Recipient Name: The person who will receive the delivery

How We Use Door Delivery Information

Your door delivery information is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and delivering your earned rewards
  • Coordinating with our delivery partners
  • Providing delivery updates and tracking information
  • Verifying delivery completion
  • Resolving any delivery-related issues

Important Delivery Information:

  • Delivery addresses are verified for accuracy before processing
  • Someone must be available at the delivery address to receive the package
  • Valid phone number is required for delivery confirmation
  • Delivery typically occurs within 7-10 working days after qualification
  • Free shipping is provided across India for all qualified rewards

Delivery Partners and Third-Party Sharing

We work with trusted delivery partners to fulfill rewards. When sharing your delivery information with these partners, we:

  • Only share the minimum information necessary for delivery
  • Require partners to maintain confidentiality
  • Ensure partners comply with data protection standards
  • Limit information sharing to delivery purposes only

Data Retention for Delivery Information

Your door delivery information is retained for:

  • Active Delivery Period: Until delivery is confirmed complete
  • Post-Delivery: 30 days for any potential delivery issues or claims
  • Archival: Basic delivery records may be kept for 1 year for program analytics

Security Note: All door delivery information is encrypted and stored securely. We never use your delivery address for marketing purposes or share it with unauthorized third parties.

Delivery Address Updates

If you need to update your delivery address or information:

  • Contact us immediately if your address changes before delivery
  • Updates can only be made before the delivery is dispatched
  • Address verification may be required for changes
