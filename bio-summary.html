Introduction

This hub provides a structured approach for mastering the NEET 2025 Biology syllabus, covering both Class 11 and Class 12 topics as per the NMC guidelines. The focus is on high-weightage chapters, smart strategies, and efficient revision.

Class 11 Biology Syllabus

Unit 1: Diversity in Living World

  • Biodiversity and its importance.
  • Taxonomy and classification systems.
  • Binomial nomenclature.
  • Five-kingdom classification.
  • Plant and animal kingdom diversity.

Unit 2: Structural Organisation in Animals and Plants

  • Plant tissues and anatomy (root, stem, leaf).
  • Animal tissues and morphology (frog example).
  • Structural adaptations.

Unit 3: Cell Structure and Function

  • Prokaryotic vs. eukaryotic cells.
  • Cell organelles and their functions.
  • Cell theory.
  • Cell division (mitosis, meiosis).

Unit 4: Plant Physiology

  • Photosynthesis, respiration, transpiration.
  • Plant growth hormones.
  • Photoperiodism.
  • Water and mineral nutrition.

Unit 5: Human Physiology

  • Digestion, respiration, and circulation.
  • Excretion and osmoregulation.
  • Neural control and coordination.
  • Endocrine system.
  • Locomotion and movement.

Class 12 Biology Syllabus

Unit 6: Reproduction

  • Asexual and sexual reproduction in plants.
  • Human reproduction and reproductive health.
  • Reproductive hormones.
  • Assisted reproductive technologies (ART).

Unit 7: Genetics and Evolution

  • Mendelian inheritance.
  • Molecular basis of inheritance (DNA, RNA).
  • DNA replication, transcription, translation.
  • Evolutionary theories and evidence.

Unit 8: Biology and Human Welfare

  • Human health and diseases.
  • Immunity and vaccines.
  • Improvement in food production.

Unit 9: Biotechnology and Its Applications

  • Principles of biotechnology.
  • Genetic engineering.
  • Applications in medicine and agriculture.
  • Ethical issues and biosafety.

Unit 10: Ecology and Environment

  • Ecosystems and energy flow.
  • Biodiversity and conservation.
  • Population and community ecology.
  • Environmental issues and pollution.

Preparation Strategy

  1. Thoroughly review NCERT Class 11 & 12 Biology textbooks.
  2. Prepare concise notes for last-minute revision.
  3. Practice PYQs (previous year questions).
  4. Attempt mock tests regularly to build speed and accuracy.
  5. Focus on high-weightage topics: Human Physiology (20%), Genetics (18%).
