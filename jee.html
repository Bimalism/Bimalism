JEE Complete Syllabus

Your comprehensive guide to Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics syllabus for JEE Main & Advanced examination. Access chapter summaries and quizzes for each topic.

Physics

Ch 1: Units and Measurements
Physical quantities, SI units, dimensional analysis, significant figures, error analysis.
Ch 2: Motion in a Straight Line
Distance, displacement, speed, velocity, acceleration, equations of motion, graphs.
Ch 3: Motion in a Plane
Vectors, projectile motion, uniform circular motion, relative velocity.
Ch 4: Laws of Motion
Newton's laws of motion, friction, dynamics of uniform circular motion.
Ch 5: Work, Energy and Power
Work, kinetic energy, work-energy theorem, power, potential energy, conservation of energy.
Ch 6: System of Particles and Rotational Motion
Center of mass, torque, angular momentum, moment of inertia, theorems of perpendicular and parallel axes.
Ch 7: Gravitation
Universal law of gravitation, acceleration due to gravity, gravitational potential energy, satellites.
Ch 8: Mechanical Properties of Solids
Elastic behavior, stress-strain relationship, Hooke's law, Young's modulus, bulk modulus, shear modulus.
Ch 9: Mechanical Properties of Fluids
Pressure, Pascal's law, Bernoulli's principle, viscosity, surface tension.
Ch 10: Thermal Properties of Matter
Heat, temperature, thermal expansion, calorimetry, heat transfer, Newton's law of cooling.
Ch 11: Thermodynamics
Thermal equilibrium, laws of thermodynamics, heat engines, refrigerators.
Ch 12: Kinetic Theory
Molecular nature of matter, behavior of gases, kinetic theory of gases, degrees of freedom.
Ch 13: Oscillations
Periodic motion, simple harmonic motion, oscillations of spring, simple pendulum.
Ch 14: Waves
Wave motion, types of waves, speed of wave, superposition principle, standing waves, beats, Doppler effect.

Chemistry

Ch 1: Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry
Matter, properties, measurement, mole concept, stoichiometry.
Ch 2: Structure of Atom
Atomic models, quantum numbers, electronic configuration, Aufbau principle.
Ch 3: Classification of Elements and Periodicity in Properties
Periodic table, periodic trends, atomic radius, ionization energy, electronegativity.
Ch 4: Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure
Ionic bonding, covalent bonding, VSEPR theory, hybridization, molecular orbital theory.
Ch 5: States of Matter
Gases, liquids, solids, intermolecular forces, gas laws, kinetic theory of gases.
Ch 6: Thermodynamics
System, surroundings, laws of thermodynamics, enthalpy, entropy, Gibbs free energy.
Ch 7: Equilibrium
Chemical equilibrium, ionic equilibrium, acids and bases, pH, buffer solutions.
Ch 8: Redox Reactions
Oxidation-reduction reactions, balancing redox reactions, electrochemical cells.
Ch 9: Hydrogen
Position of hydrogen in periodic table, isotopes, preparation, properties, and uses of hydrogen.
Ch 10: s-Block Elements
Alkali and alkaline earth metals, electronic configuration, occurrence, anomalous properties.

Mathematics

Ch 1: Sets, Relations and Functions
Sets and their representations, types of relations and functions, composition of functions.
Ch 2: Complex Numbers and Quadratic Equations
Complex numbers, algebra of complex numbers, modulus and argument, quadratic equations.
Ch 3: Matrices and Determinants
Matrices, algebra of matrices, determinants, properties of determinants, adjoint and inverse of matrix.
Ch 4: Permutations and Combinations
Fundamental principle of counting, permutations, combinations, simple applications.
Ch 5: Mathematical Induction
Principle of mathematical induction and simple applications.
Ch 6: Binomial Theorem
Binomial theorem for positive integral indices, general term, middle term, simple applications.
Ch 7: Sequences and Series
Arithmetic and geometric progressions, arithmetic mean, geometric mean, sum to n terms of special series.
Ch 8: Limits, Continuity and Differentiability
Limits, continuity, differentiability, derivative of composite functions, chain rule.
Ch 9: Integral Calculus
Integration as inverse process of differentiation, definite integrals, properties, fundamental theorem of calculus.
Ch 10: Differential Equations
Ordinary differential equations, formation of differential equations, solutions of differential equations.

Ch 11: Coordinate Geometry
Straight lines, circles, conic sections, parabola, ellipse, hyperbola.

Ch 12: Three Dimensional Geometry
Direction cosines and ratios, equation of a line in space, angle between two lines, shortest distance between two lines.
Ch 13: Vector Algebra
Vectors and scalars, magnitude and direction of a vector, types of vectors, addition of vectors.
Ch 14: Statistics and Probability
Measures of dispersion, probability, random experiments, events, axiomatic approach to probability.
Ch 15: Trigonometry
Trigonometric functions, identities, equations, inverse trigonometric functions.
