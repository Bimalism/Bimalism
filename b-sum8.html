Microbes in Human Welfare

1. Health, Diseases & Immunology

  • Health = state of complete physical, mental & social well-being; disease = malfunctioning.
  • Malaria – Plasmodium vivax/falciparum, female Anopheles mosquito, life cycle involves liver & RBC.
  • Filariasis (elephantiasis) – Wuchereria bancrofti, female Culex mosquito, blocks lymph vessels.
  • Ascariasis – Ascaris lumbricoides (roundworm), faecal-oral route, lives in intestine.
  • Typhoid – Salmonella typhi, Widal test, fever, weakness, intestinal perforation possible.
  • Pneumonia – Streptococcus pneumoniae/Haemophilus influenzae, alveoli filled with fluid.
  • Common cold – Rhino virus, nasal chamber, highly infectious.
  • Amoebiasis – Entamoeba histolytica, contaminated water, dysentery.
  • Ringworm – Microsporum/Trichophyton, warm & moist skin, itching.
  • Cancer – uncontrolled cell division, carcinogens, metastasis; treatment – surgery, radiation, chemotherapy, immunotherapy.

2. Microbes in Human Welfare

  • Household: curd (Lactobacillus), dough (yeast – CO₂), idli/dosa (fermentation by bacteria & yeast).
  • Industrial: antibiotics (Penicillium notatum – penicillin), fermented beverages (yeast – ethanol).
  • Sewage treatment: primary (physical) → secondary (aerobic bacteria in aeration tank → anaerobic sludge digesters → biogas (methane).
  • Biogas plant: methanogens (Methanobacterium) in cow-dung produce biogas (60–70% CH₄).
  • Biofertilizers: Rhizobium (symbiotic N₂ fixer), Azospirillum/Anabaena (free-living), mycorrhiza, cyanobacter (paddy fields).
  • Biocontrol: Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt cotton), Trichoderma (fungal biocontrol), ladybird & dragonflies (aphid/mosquito control).
  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM) – minimal chemical pesticides + biological control.
  • Microbes as biofertilizers reduce chemical fertilizer use, eco-friendly.
  • Cyclosporin A (immunosuppressant) from Trichoderma polysporum; Statins (blood cholesterol lowering) from Monascus purpureus.
  • Microbes also used in production of enzymes, organic acids, vitamins, single-cell protein (SCP).
